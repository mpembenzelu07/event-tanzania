Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

What's included in your event planning packages? +
Our packages include professional event planning services tailored to your needs. This typically includes initial consultation, vendor recommendations and management, budget planning, timeline creation, and day-of coordination. Higher-tier packages include additional services like full design concepts, unlimited vendor meetings, and post-event services.
Do you offer custom packages? +
Yes, we understand that every event is unique. We offer custom packages tailored to your specific requirements and budget. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your event details, guest count, and desired services.
What is your payment structure? +
We require a 30% deposit to secure your event date. The remaining balance is divided into two payments: 50% due 60 days before the event, and the final 20% due 14 days before the event. For events booked with less than 60 days' notice, a modified payment schedule will be arranged.
